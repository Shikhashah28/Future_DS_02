# Future_DS_02
# Social Media Campaign Performance Tracker

## Project Overview
- This project analyzes marketing campaign performance across multiple advertising channels using Power BI
- The objective is to evaluate how advertising spend contributes to product sales
- The dashboard converts raw CSV data into actionable business insights to support data-driven decision making

---

## Dataset Description
- The dataset contains advertising spend across multiple marketing channels and total products sold
- Columns included:
  - TV
  - Billboards
  - Google Ads
  - Social Media
  - Influencer Marketing
  - Affiliate Marketing
  - Product_Sold
- Each row represents aggregated campaign-level data

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall advertising spend and campaign performance
- Compare effectiveness across different marketing channels
- Measure sales efficiency and ROI proxy metrics
- Identify high-performing and underperforming channels
- Provide actionable recommendations for future marketing campaigns

---

## Key Metrics and Measures
- Total Advertising Spend
- Total Products Sold
- Sales Efficiency (Products Sold per Unit Spend)
- Channel-wise ROI Proxy (Products Sold divided by Channel Spend)

**Note:**  
ROI Proxy is used because revenue data by channel is not available. This is a commonly used efficiency metric in marketing analytics.

---

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards providing a high-level overview of campaign performance
- Channel-wise advertising spend analysis
- Spend versus performance comparison visuals
- ROI and efficiency ranking across marketing channels
- Interactive and business-focused visual design

---

## Tools and Technologies Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- CSV data source

---

## Key Business Insights
- Digital marketing channels demonstrate higher sales efficiency compared to traditional media
- High-spend channels with low efficiency indicate opportunities for budget optimization
- Influencer and Affiliate Marketing show strong return potential relative to spend
- Reallocating budget toward high-efficiency channels can improve overall campaign performance

---

## Outcome
- Enables stakeholders to understand how marketing spend translates into product sales
- Supports informed budget allocation decisions
- Helps identify opportunities to optimize future campaigns
